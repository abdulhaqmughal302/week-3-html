1. Classic Spaghetti Bolognese

A rich and hearty Italian pasta dish loved by everyone.

Ingredients

  • 250g spaghetti
  • 250g minced beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Boil spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté onion and garlic.
  3. Add minced beef and cook until browned.
  4. Add canned tomatoes, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. Serve sauce over spaghetti and top with parmesan cheese.

2. Simple Vegetable Salad

A fresh and healthy salad that goes with any meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups lettuce, chopped
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 1 carrot, grated
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Wash and chop all vegetables.
  2. Combine lettuce, cucumber, tomatoes, and carrot in a bowl.
  3. Drizzle olive oil and lemon juice over the salad.
  4. Add salt to taste and toss well before serving.

3. Chocolate Chip Cookies

Soft, chewy, and perfect for dessert or snack time.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Beat butter and sugar together until creamy.
  3. Add eggs and mix well.
  4. Stir in flour and baking soda, then fold in chocolate chips.
  5. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto a baking sheet.
  6. Bake for 10-12 minutes until golden brown.
