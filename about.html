About FitTrack

FitTrack was created to make fitness tracking simple and accessible for everyone, from beginners to seasoned athletes.

Our Mission

We believe consistency is the key to fitness success. Our mission is to give you the tools to stay consistent, track your progress, and stay motivated on your journey.

Our Story

FitTrack started as a simple workout log and grew into a complete fitness tracking platform used by people all over the world to reach their health and fitness goals.

What We Offer

  • Workout logging and history
  • Progress charts and statistics
  • Custom goal setting
  • Reminders to keep you on track
